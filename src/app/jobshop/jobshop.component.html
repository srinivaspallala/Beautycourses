JOB SHOP – FOR EMPLOYERS


Staffing is key to any business, but finding the right match of skills to your particular needs is usually costly, with agencies and advertising taking a large slice of your money and time. That’s where the LCBT Job Shop comes in; we have been offering a free recruitment service in a wide variety of sectors since 2000, placing thousands of candidates into work direct through us. This is a free service we offer to employers which matches your vacancy with hundreds of qualified students. If you have a vacancy in beauty, retail, customer service or front of house that you want us to advertise then follow the simple process:


  • Download the job vacancy form from HERE
  • Fully complete the job vacancy form
  • Then either email the form to jobshop@lcbt.co.uk or click on the “Register your Vacancy” button below which should open up your send email.
  • The job vacancy will be checked before posting on the website and being promoted to students. You may be called or email in regards to the job vacancy if there are any queries regarding.
