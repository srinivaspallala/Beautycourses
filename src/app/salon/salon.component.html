THE SALON AT LCBT

The LCBT Salon open but with only limited treatments. Please contact our Salon for more information.


Our LCBT Salon, based in our Ramillies Street site, just off Oxford Circus and opposite Fitness First, allows you to experience expert salon treatments at a fraction of the cost compared to high street prices!

We offer a wide range of services that you would expect in any other salon – using a wide range of products which include Kaeso, Dermalogica, Yon-Ka, OPI and EVO Bio sculpture Gel.

Our student therapists perform the treatments once they have successfully completed the necessary training, which provides them commercial experience to support them on their journey into the Hair, Make-Up and Beauty Industries. Salon sessions are fully supported and monitored by a qualified lecturer.

For a more advanced service, our Level 3 students are able to upgrade your facial by using Dermalogica products, which will leave your skin feeling and looking fantastic!

We also offer Level 4 Aesthetic facial treatments by using Mesoesthetic products, Renew tired looking skin by increasing collagen production to repair scars.

Please note that as we are a training salon, not all treatments are available at all times – so please ring us to check availability.

A small percentage of our student therapists are male, so please let us know if you will require a female therapist to perform your treatment.
