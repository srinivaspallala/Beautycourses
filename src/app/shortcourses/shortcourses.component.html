SHORT COURSES

If you’re looking to gain a new qualification, upskill or get some extra CPD, LCBT's short courses are designed to do just that. On offer throughout the year, you’ll receive a certificate and be eligible for insurance upon completion to start practicing your new skills in your workplace and add the qualifications to your CV.

In addition to offering our very own short courses, LCBT have also become a Sterex Academy and CIDESCO school delivering a range of advanced qualifications. LCBT's Sterex Academy courses allow you to build on skills gained from a Level 3 Beauty Therapy Diploma and develop yourself both professionally and personally, learning a range of advanced electrolysis techniques which will leave you ready for employment in a clinical environment. The CIBTAC Level 4 Aesthetic qualifications are designed to be flexible, giving a blended learning approach through audio and visual e-learning and minimal contact days. Each course allows you to build your qualifications gradually and in accordance with your individual needs.
